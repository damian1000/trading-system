◆ TRADING SYSTEM privacy notice

Privacy

This site keeps a standard web-server access log of each request. From it, aggregate usage is derived: approximate location (city and country, from the IP address), network operator (ASN), browser and operating-system family, referrer, and the pages and API endpoints used.

The IP address itself is not stored. At ingestion it is replaced with a salted one-way hash, so repeat visits can be counted without retaining the address. The site sets no cookies and loads no client-side trackers or third-party analytics.

Records are deleted automatically after 90 days.

The positions shown are resettable market data from a demonstration book, not a record of anyone's holdings.

Legal basis: legitimate interest in understanding use of a personal portfolio site. Questions are welcome via an issue on the repository.
